    Hemp Seed Alfredo Pasta

      Don’t know what to do with a bag of hemp seeds?

    I heard hemp seeds are an amazing superfood to add to smoothie bowls. Have you had hemp seeds before? To me, that nutty taste doesn’t go well with my fruity smoothie so I didn’t know what to do with a whole bag of hemp seeds. I googled ways to use it and this was by far my best discovery… Hemp Seed Alfredo Sauce! Instead of using the typical vegan cheese or cauliflower, we use hemp seeds and cashews. Hemp seeds are packed with nutrients and have a bland enough flavor to easily be incorporated into many dishes.

    HEMP SEED ALFREDO PASTA

    Time:30 mins
    Difficulty: Medium
    Servings:1
    Ingredients
    • Pasta
    • Sauce:
      • 1 Roasted red bell pepper
      • 1/2 Red onion
      • 1 cloves of Garlic
      • 1/4 cup Cashews
      • 0.50 cup Unsweetened almond or soy milk
      • 1 Tblsp nutritional yeast
      • 1/4 tsp salt
      • Black pepper, to taste
      • Red pepper flakes, to taste
      • Vegetables:
        • Vegetables: Use any, but these are what I had in my fridge:
        • Corn
        • Mushroom
        • Cauliflower
      • Sautee ingredients:
        • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh sage
        • Rosemary
        • Chopped fresh parsley
    Instructions
    1. Cook the pasta according to the instructions on the package. Set aside.
    2. Put all the sauce ingredients in a food processor and process until smooth.
    3. In a sauce pan, sautee the vegetables you would like to use on medium heat for a couple of minutes.
    4. Pour the red bell pepper sauce into a saucepan.
    5. Add in red pepper flakes and pepper to taste. Cook until sauce starts to bubble.
    6. Stir in the cooked fettuccine and serve immediately. Sprinkle with fresh chopped parsley and more red pepper flakes if you like.
