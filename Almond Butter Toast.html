    Almond Butter Toast

      Simplicity can still be good

    This is what I make on a lazy day, it only takes 2 minutes to whip up. For best results, make your own almond butter like how I did!

    ALMOND BUTTER TOAST

    Time:2 mins
    Difficulty:Easy
    Servings:1
    Ingredients
    • 2 slices of Bread
    • 1/2 Banana, Sliced
    • Almond Butter
    • Cinnamon
      • Cacao Nibs
      • Other fruits
      • Sliced Almonds
    Instructions
    • Spread the almond butter on toasted bread. Layer on bananas and sprinkle on cinnamon. Top with whatever toppings you want.
