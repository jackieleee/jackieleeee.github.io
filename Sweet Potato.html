    Sweet Potato Boats

      Trying to cut down on the complex carbs?

    Try making sweet potato boats or even cut them into thin slices and toast them to make sweet potato toast. This recipe is simple, and the only thing that takes the longest is just roasting the sweet potatoes. The rest only takes a few minutes of chopping, stirring, and placing! Here are two variations of toppings for you to try, a sweet and a savory recipe. Both super healthy, vegan, and flavorful.

    SWEET POTATO BOATS

    Time: 50 mins
    Difficulty: Easy
    Servings: 1
    Ingredients
    • 1 large Sweet Potato
    • 1 tblsp Beet Horseradish
    • Cilantro
    • 3 tomatoes
    • 2 strawberries
    • 2 tblsp Vegan Tofutti Sour Cream
    • 1 tblsp maple syrup
    Instructions
    1. Preheat oven to 400F
    2. Poke the sweet potatoes all over with a fork
    3. Bake for 45 mins until tender
    4. While baking, mix together sour cream and maple syrup.
    5. When Sweet Potatoes are down, cut it in half and let it cool down for a few minutes.
    6. Top one half with the sour cream mixture and top with strawberries (and cocoa nibs if you have).
    7. Top the other half of the sweet potato with the beet horseradish, cilantro, and tomatoes.
