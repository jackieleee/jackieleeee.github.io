    Strawberries & Cream Smoothie Bowl

      Start the Day Off with a Colorful Breakfast

    This is not your standard Strawberry smoothie bowl. You can definitely taste the CREAM in this from the frozen banana. The vanilla protein powder gives the flavor an extra boost and also helps you build muscles! Don’t have vanilla protein powder? Just skip it and use vanilla stevia or vanilla extract and more banana!

    STRAWBERRIES & CREAM SMOOTHIE BOWL

    Time:10 mins
    Difficulty: Easy
    Servings:1
    Ingredients
    • Smoothie:
      • 1 frozen Banana
      • 1 cup Frozen Strawberries
      • 1 cup Frozen Raspberries
      • 1/2 tsp Natural Sweetener
      • 1/4 cup Vanilla Unsweetened Almond Milk (or you can use coconut milk for an even more tropical flavor)
    • Optional: 1 scoop plant-based Vanilla Protein Powder – I use Tropeaka
    • Toppings:
      • Granola
      • Desiccated coconut
      • Hemp seeds
      • Chopped fruits (I used pineapples)
    Instructions
    • Blend all Smoothie ingredients with high powered processing blender until well blended and top with the toppings of your choice!
