    Chickpea Nourish Bowl

      Eat the rainbow

    I love Sweet Greens, but I hate the feeling of having to pay $13 for a bowl of salad! If I could make my own amazing nourish bowl and make it taste even better, why not! I meal prepped the sweet potato so this salad normally takes me only 5-7 minutes to put together. If you like to make this even heartier, add a bed of quinoa or rice. Meal prepping sweet potatoes are always a good idea. It can save you a ton of time for the remainder of the week.

    CHICKPEA NOURISH BOWL

    Time: 45 mins
    Difficulty: Medium
    Servings: 1
    Ingredients
    • 1/4 large sweet potato, I roasted the whole sweet potato and kept the rest in the fridge as meal prep â€“ cut into small chunks
    • Chickpea mixture:
      • 1/4 avocado, in chunks
      • 1/5 onion, chopped
      • 1/5 can chickpeas, washed and drained
      • 1/8 tsp garam masala
      • 1/8 tsp garlic powder
      • 1/8 tsp cumin
      • salt & pepper to taste
      • 2 tblsp capers
      • lime juice
    • 1/2 roasted red pepper bell pepper, cut in small slices
    • Tahini
    • Hot sauce
    • Beet saurkraut
    • Cilantro
    • Dressing: Makes 1 cup
      • 3/4 cup EVOO
      • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
      • 2 tblsp maple syrup
      • 2 tsp dijon mustard
      • 1/4 tsp black pepper
      • 1/4 tsp paprika
      • 1/8 tsp garlic
      • Pinch salt
      Instructions
      1. Preheat oven to 400 F
      2. Cut up sweet potato into large chunks and bake for 40 mins
      3. While sweet potato is roasting, build your bowl.
      4. Layer a bed of mixed greens in your bowl
      5. In a separate bowl, mix together ingredients in chickpea mixture.
      6. In another bowl, mix together dressing ingredients. (This dressing makes a bigger serving than you need, but you can keep it for several days in the fridge)
      7. Pour some dressing over the bed of greens (not too much!)
      8. Add chickpea mixture, roasted red bell pepper, beet sauerkraut, cilantro, and sweet potatoes. Drizzle tahini over sweet potato.
