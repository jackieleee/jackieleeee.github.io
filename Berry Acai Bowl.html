    Berry Acai Bowl

      This is the best breakfast for a hot summer day

    This is what gets me out of bed. An amazing smoothie bowl, that I look forward to the second I wake up! Even better… the granola is homemade too. Granola recipe coming soon!
    This recipe is so diverse, if you don’t have acai, just use another cup of frozen fruits. Any kind of fruit! If you want to look for a substitute for acai, use mixed berries. The acai I use is from nativo acai. The only thing is, when you order it from nativo, the smallest amount you can order is 28 packs (28 servings). That’s crazy!! If you just want a few packs, check your nearest trader joes, whole foods, or health foods store.

    BERRY ACAI BOWL

    Time:10 mins
    Difficulty:Easy
    Servings:1
    Ingredients
    • Smoothie
      • 1/2 Banana
      • 1 pack of Acai
      • 1 cup of Frozen Mixed Berries (Pineapple, Peach, Strawberry, Raspberry)
      • 1/2 tsp Vanilla Extract (I use liquid Vanilla Stevia)
      • 1/4 cup Vanilla Unsweetened Almond Milk
    • Toppings
      • Granola
      • Cacao Nibs
      • Chopped fruits
      Instructions
      • Blend all Smoothie ingredients with high powered processing blender until well blended and top with the toppings of your choice!
