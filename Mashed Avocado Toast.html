    Mashed Avocado Toast

      Craving something savory in the morning?

    This is one of my go-to breakfasts. This is not a typical avocado toast. The dash of cumin adds a LOT of flavor to this dish. I typically also try to use kelp granules, instead of salt, to get in that extra iodine. If you can’t find kelp granules, don’t worry, just use sea salt to taste.

    MASHED AVOCADO TOAST

    Time:5 mins
    Difficulty:Easy
    Servings:1
    Ingredients
    • 2 slices of Bread, toasted
    • 1/4 Avocado
    • Lemon juice
    • Spinach or Arugula (or both)
    • Pepper
    • Kelp flakes (or salt)
    • Cumin
    • 3 Cherry tomatoes, sliced
    Instructions
    • Toast the bread
    • While you wait for the bread to toast, mash the avocado with a fork. Add lemon juice, pepper, kelp flakes (or salt), and cumin to taste.
    • Once toast is ready, add spinach or arugula (or both) on top. Spread mashed avocado on top of greens and add tomatoes. Lay second slice of toast on top.
