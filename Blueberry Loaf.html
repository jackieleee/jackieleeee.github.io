    BLUEBERRY LOAF

      Easy Breakfast Loaf

    Love blueberries? This quick bread is just for you. This recipe calls for applesauce, which is a great substitute for eggs, oil, butter, yogurt. It was my first time using it, and it definitely makes cooking easier. (Don’t have to make flax/chia egg).
    Original Recipe from Two Raspberries

    BLUEBERRY LOAF

    Time:1 hour
    Difficulty: Medium
    Servings:6-9
    Ingredients
    • Dry Ingredients
      • 1½ cup all-purpose unbleached flour
      • 2 tsp baking powder
      • ½ tsp sea salt
      • Wet Ingredients
        • ½ cup unsweetened vanilla almond milk
        • ½ tsp apple cider vinegar
        • ½ cup vegan cane sugar + more for topping
        • 3 oz unsweetened natural applesauce
        • ¼ cup vegan butter melted
        • 1 tsp vanilla
        • ¾ cup blueberries (I used frozen) plus more for topping
      Instructions
      1. Pre-heat the oven at 375 F
      2. In a small bowl add your almond milk and apple cider vinegar and set aside.
      3. In a large bowl add all your dry ingredients.
      4. In a separate small bowl melt your butter, add melted butter and almond milk mixture to the dry ingredients and continue adding the rest of the wet ingredients applesauce and vanilla.
      5. MIX until everything in well combined. But try not to over mix or beat it! Lastly, add blueberries and gently fold them in.
      6. Spray a loaf dish and pour the mixture in there, top with extra blueberries and cane sugar
      7. Place in the oven for 37 minutes.
      8. Check with toothpick
