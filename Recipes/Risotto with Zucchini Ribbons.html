    Risotto with Zucchini Ribbons

      Gourmet meal in 30 minutes

    This recipe has changed the way I cook my rice. Never thought cooking rice in broth would be so delicious! I always thought Risotto would require dairy, but I skipped the dairy and it was still so yummy.

    RISOTTO WITH ZUCCHINI RIBBONS

    Time:1 hr
    Difficulty: Medium
    Servings:2
    Ingredients
    • 2 Shallots
    • 5 tsp Balsamic Vinegar
    • 2 tsp Olive Oil
    • 1 tbsp Vegan butter
    • 2 Veggie Stock Concentrates
    • 2 Cloves Garlic
    • 1 lemon
    • 1 Zucchini
    • 3/4 Cup Arborio Rice
    • 1/4 Nutritional Yeast
    • 10 cherry tomatos
    • Mixed Greens
    Instructions
    1. Preheat oven to 400 F.
    2. Peel and quarter shallots, then place on a large piece of aluminum foil.
    3. Lift sides of foil and crimp to make a bowl
    4. Toss in balsamic vinegar, a drizzle of olive oil, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
    5. Place on a baking sheet
    6. Roast for 15-20 mins
    7. Bring 3 cups water and stock concnetrates to a gentle simmer in a small pot
    8. Mince or grate garlic
    9. Halve lemon
    10. Uinsg a vegetable peeler, shave zucchini into ribbons, running blade down the length.
    11. Stop shaving once you get to the seedy core. Finely chop core.
    12. Melt 1 tblsp Vegan Butter in a pot. Add garlic and zucchini core.
    13. Cook, tossing, until softened (about 3 mins)
    14. Add rice and cook, stirring, until grains are translucent (1-2mins)
    15. Lower meat to medium-low heat. Add stock 1/2 cup at a time to pan with rice, stirring after each addtion. Allow rice to absorb most of stock before adding more. Continue until grains are al dente and creamy (25-30mins)
    16. Season with salt and pepper
    17. While risotto cooks, heat a drizzle of olive oil in another large pan over medium heat. Add zucchini ribbons. Season with salt and pepper. Cook tossing, until tender and softened, about 5 minutes. Add a squeeze of lemon and toss.
    18. Stir shallots and nutritional yeast into risotto. Season with salt and pepper. Divide risotto between plates placing on top of a bed of greens and top with zucchini ribbons and tomatos.
