    Olive & Sun-dried tomato Tapenade

      Looking to feed a ton of people at a potluck??

    This clean and healthy dish can feed a TON of people. The saltiness of the olives balances out with the sweetness of the sun-dried tomatoes. Toast the french bread for an extra crunch. You can even use this tapenade mix and put it into a salad, it’ll be just as delicious as on bread.
    Original Recipe from It Doesn’t Taste Like Chicken

    OLIVE & SUN-DRIED TOMATO TAPENADE

    Time:15 mins
    Difficulty: Easy
    Servings:10
    Ingredients
    • French bread
    • 1 Cup Mixed Pitted Olives
    • ¾ Cup Sun-Dried Tomatoes
    • ¼ Cup Fresh Basil Leaves
    • 1 Clove Garlic
    • 2 teaspoons Lemon Zest
    • 2 teaspoons Lemon Juice
    • Salt & Pepper to taste if desired
    Instructions
    1. Taste your olives, if too salty, soak or rinse in water before using.
    2. Toast french bread (if using)
    3. Once your olives are ready, remove pits, and chop them and add them to a bowl. Add in chopped sun-dried tomatoes, basil, and finely minced garlic to the bowl. Stir in the zest and juice of the lemon.
    4. Mix it all up, and taste. Add more lemon, salt and pepper if desired. Serve along with toast or crackers. Or add into salads.
