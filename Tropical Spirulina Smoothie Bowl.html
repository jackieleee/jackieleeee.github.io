      New to spirulina? TAKE IT SLOW!

    I recently purchased some spirulina powder but didnâ€™t know what to use it for. I tried making a spirulina smoothie a few days before, but the spirulina flavor was a little too much for me. The spirulina powder tastes like seaweed, and that smoothie bowl tasted exactly like that. I did some more research and learned to add in the spirulina in small amounts. In this recipe, I only used 1/2 tsp and I think it was perfect!

    TROPICAL SPIRULINA SMOOTHIE BOWL

    Time:10 mins
    Difficulty:Easy
    Servings:1
    Ingredients
    • Smoothie
      • 1 frozen Banana
      • 2 cups frozen pineapple
      • 1/2 tsp Natural Sweetener
      • 1/4 cup Vanilla Unsweetened Almond Milk (or you can use coconut milk for an even more tropical flavor)
      • 1/2 tsp spirulina powder
    • Toppings
      • Granola
      • Desiccated coconut
      • Dried mulberries
      • Chopped fruits (I used strawberries)
      Instructions
      • Blend all Smoothie ingredients with high powered processing blender until well blended and top with the toppings of your choice!
