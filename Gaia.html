      Experience

      Gaia Group

     

      Gaia Group owns and operates a string of western fine dining, casual dining and Chinese restaurants. Over the past 18 years the Group has grown to a portfolio of over 40 restaurants with multiple themes in different locations in Hong Kong, Shanghai, Beijing, and Bangkok. The Group continues to expand in Asia every day incorporating new food concepts and modern designs.

       

      These past few months, ever since I graduated I've started working at Gaia Group. This job has brought me a lot of expierence from social media account management, website design, hosting KOL's, event planning, etc. to menu layout design, promotional flyer typewriting, and more. I was able to get my hands on a range of tasks and learn a lot from the people I was working with and the environment I was put in.

       

      During my time there, I got to see the company expand into brand new restaurants. I got to work with brand new concepts as well as franchises that were constantly evolving and redesigned. I was in charge of the branding and the consumer facing side of marketing for Gaia Group.
