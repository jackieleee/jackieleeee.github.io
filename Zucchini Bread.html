    ZUCCHINI BREAD

    Time:1 hour
    Difficulty: Medium
    Servings:8
    Ingredients
    • 3 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
    • 1/2 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
    • 1 and 1/2 cups granulated sugar
    • 1 cup unsweetened applesauce
    • 1/2 cup coconut oil, melted
    • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
    • 2 finely grated zucchini
    • 1 and 1/2 cups vegan chocolate chips
    Instructions
    1. Prepare loaf pan with oil
    2. In a large bowl combine the flour, baking soda, baking powder. cinnamon, and nutmeg; mix well to combine and set aside.
    3. In a separate bowl combine the granulated sugar, applesauce, coconut oil, and vanilla extract.
    4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry and stir, using a rubber spatula, until barely combined.
    5. Add the zucchini and mix with the spatula until combined.
    6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
    7. Fill pan up with batter
