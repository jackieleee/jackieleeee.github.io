    Banana Apple Bread

      Challenge yourself and make bread from scratch!

    This recipe came out not too sweet and is perfect to satisfy your sweet tooth craving without leaving making you feel like you had a heavy dessert. To make it sweeter, adding dried fruits or more chocolate chips would be a great idea. This is a super easy and fun recipe to try when you are bored at home with nothing to do. This recipe doesn’t call for too many things but hopefully, you have access to some ripe bananas!

    BANANA APPLE BREAD

    Time:1 hour
    Difficulty: Medium
    Servings:8
    Ingredients
    • 1¾ cup flour
    • 2¼ tsp baking powder
    • ¼ tsp baking soda
    • 1 tsp cinnamon (I did a mix of¾ tsp cinnamon and¼ tsp nutmeg)
    • ¼ tsp salt
    • 3 to 4 Tbsp fine sugar or sweetener
    • ½ cup non-dairy milk
    • 2 large ripe bananas
    • 1 Tbsp blackstrap molasses
    • 2 Tbsp nut butter
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • 1 cup grated apple (1 regular size apple shredded)
    • Optional:
      • A few Tbsps walnuts, pecans, vegan chocolate chips, dried fruit, chopped dates or other additions
    Instructions
    1. Preheat oven: 365 F
    2. In a bowl whisk together the dry ingredients (flour to sugar)
    3. In a blender, add banana, non-dairy milk, nut butter, molasses, and vanilla. Blend until smooth. Add to the bowl of dry ingredients, and mix until well combined. Add grated apple and optional toppings.
      • Alternatively, you could mash the banana with a fork until smooth then add in the non-dairy milk, nut butter, and molasses.
    4. Transfer batter to greased loaf pan. Sprinkle chopped nuts and chocolate chips on top (optional).
    5. Bake for 40 minutes at 365 F.
    6. Reduce heat to 350 F and Bake for 10 – 15 minutes
      • Use a toothpick or chopstick to check if the center is done cooking
    7. Cool for 10 minutes in the pan
    8. Remove from the pan and cool completely before slicing.
