    VEGAN CHEESE

    Even better than real cheese

These cheese tastes more like cream cheese and works great as a spread on French Bread. This recipe makes the base of the cheese, and you can add whatever herbs or toppings you want. You might even want to try making a pistachio crust but crushing pistachio nuts and sprinkling them on top as I did with some herbs. Each one of these cheeses made had very different flavors. The one I did not make a top crust for, was the cashew based cheese, the other two were almond. To my surprise, the cashew cheese was a crowd favorite! But before you take out the nuts, you need to make Rejuvelac (super easy, don’t worry – it just takes a while to let the grains sprout).
