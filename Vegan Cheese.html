    VEGAN CHEESE

      Even better than real cheese

    These cheese tastes more like cream cheese and works great as a spread on French Bread. This recipe makes the base of the cheese, and you can add whatever herbs or toppings you want. You might even want to try making a pistachio crust but crushing pistachio nuts and sprinkling them on top as I did with some herbs. Each one of these cheeses made had very different flavors. The one I did not make a top crust for, was the cashew based cheese, the other two were almond. To my surprise, the cashew cheese was a crowd favorite! But before you take out the nuts, you need to make Rejuvelac (super easy, don’t worry – it just takes a while to let the grains sprout).

    ALMOND CREAM CHEESE

    Time: 4-5 days
    Difficulty: Easy
    Servings: 3 wheels
    Ingredients
    • 3 cups nuts (almonds or cashews) – (soaked overnight)
    • 1/2 cup rejuvelac* (See note below for recipe)
    • pinch of salt
    Instructions
    1. Soak the nuts overnight
    2. Peel off the skin – I found it easier to peel by filling up big bowl of water and submerging my hands underwater while peeling
    3. Blend nuts with salt, adding in rejuvelac
    4. Wrap into cheesecloth and place on top of strainer for a day or two so the liquids are filtered out
    5. Split into 2 portions and add in herbs, i did three different mixtures:
    6. 1 tblsp of nutritional yeast, italian herbs, garlic powder
    7. 1 tblsp of nutritional yeast, sundried tomatoes, garlic powder
    8. 1 tblsp of nutritional yeast, garlic powder
    9. Let it sit in the fridge for a few days, the longer you keep it in the fridge the most flavorful it will be.
    *Rejuvelac: Take 1 cup of grains, I used quinoa and soak it in water for a few hours, then rinse out the water, wash the grains, and fill it up again so that the water covers the grains. Do this another time the following day, but this time fill up the water to the top of the container you are using. Leave this for 2 days. On the fourth day, the liquid you have is Rejuvelac. – This process sprouts the grains and the acidic liquid is what you need to make vegan cheese.
